OUR EVENTS

Desi Night

Join us for a vibrant introduction to MSU UIISA at our much-awaited welcome event! An evening brimming with excitement, it's the perfect opportunity for new students to dive into our Indian cultural organization. Engage in fun-filled activities, forge new connections, and kick-start your journey with us in a lively, welcoming atmosphere.

Diwali

Get ready for a blast of joy and colors at our yearly Diwali bash! Picture yourself relishing delicious Indian delicacies and dancing your heart out to the beats of Indian music. Our past Diwali events have been all about scrumptious food, non-stop dancing, and an abundance of festive vibes. Join us for another unforgettable celebration this year!

Holi

Immerse yourself in the exhilarating festivities of Holi at MSU UIISA's annual celebration! Experience the joy of colors as you dance to lively beats and indulge in traditional Indian snacks. Join us for a day of vibrant fun, where the campus comes alive with laughter, dancing, and the spirit of togetherness.

Club Rush

Unleash your inner party enthusiast at Club Rush, an exclusive night event that's the epitome of electrifying entertainment! Join us for a night of non-stop music, dancing, and a club-like atmosphere. Get ready to groove to the beats, socialize, and make memories that will set the tone for an unforgettable year with MSU UIISA.

Badminton Tournament

Join the thrill of our annual Badminton Tournament! Open to all students, it's a smashing opportunity to showcase your skills, make new friends, and compete for exciting prizes. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just picking up a racket, this event promises an exhilarating day of sport and camaraderie.

Meet and Greet

We were honored to host a memorable Meet and Greet with Ankur Warikoo, a distinguished MSU alum and accomplished Indian entrepreneur. The event offered a unique opportunity for attendees to connect, learn, and be inspired by his incredible journey from campus to the world of entrepreneurship.
